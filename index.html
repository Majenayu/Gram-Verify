Aadhaar Card Information

What is Aadhaar?

Aadhaar is a 4-digit unique identity number issued by the Government of India to rural residents. It serves as proof of identity and address for accessing government services in rural areas.

Benefits of Aadhaar

  • Universal identity proof across rural India
  • Easy access to government welfare schemes
  • Simplified KYC process for banking and telecom services
  • Digital life certificate for pensioners
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) for various government schemes

Important Points

  • Keep your demographic and biometric information updated
  • You can update your Aadhaar details online
  • Link your Aadhaar card with your bank account for direct benefit transfers and loans
  • Protect your Aadhaar number as you would protect any identity document
